Lakshmi Narayan Mandir is an ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Bhagwan Shri Lakshmi-Narayan (Shri Hari). The temple is located in the proximity of B-2 & B-3 Block, Keshav Puram, Delhi, India. The upper level of the temple has the idol of Lakshmi-Narayan with the idol of Shri Ganesha, Hanuman, Maa Durga, Ram Parivaar, Radhey-Krishna, Shiv Parivaar, Maa Santoshi Mata, Maa Saraswati, Sai Baba. There are idols of Shiva-Linga, Shani & Khatu-Shyam at the same level. At the ground level, the Satsang-Bhawan is available where people perform Puja. There is also a mesmersing shiv mandir alongside for performing Shiv puja in the adjoining area at the upper level itself.
